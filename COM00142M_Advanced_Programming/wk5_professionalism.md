Self-driving cars
* Ethical questions - how does the car act as it approaches a crossing
* A number of tiny decisions made by a human every second that needs to be replicated
* Numerous deaths in selfdriving cars - people don't feel safe
* Partial self-driving exists (such as advanced driver-assist systems) that might aid a driver
* 6-level classification system
    * (0) No automation - full control by the driver, can be enhanced by warning/intervention systems
    * (1) Driver Assistance - mode-specific controls by assistance system, steering OR speed
    * (2) Partial Automation - mode-specific execution by assistance for steering AND speed
    * (3) Conditional Automation - Driver must respond to requests to intervene by a system
    * (4) High Automation - Without driver intervention, the car can stop safely
    * (5) Full automation - The system controls the car under all conditions

Unlike more limited environments, the public roads pose an extreme amount of challenge for the
corporations working on developing self-driving vehicles, and the high-profile nature of every 
fault leaves the public low on trust with using these vehicles. A study in early 2025 shows that 
only "22% of road users would trust a driverless car and feel comfortable driving in one"[1], which
is suggested to be up, and differs across age and region. It appears from personal observation that
more vehicles are starting to include smaller partially-automated controls as they slowly creep up
the automationm levels accepted by the United nations[2], which in the UK has been encouraged 
by the Automated Vehicles Act 2024[3], which claims to have "Raod safety... at the heart of the 
legislation" that has already led to self-driving trials in London and Oxford, with the intention
of supporting and growing the UK economy. Although it should be noted that the manufacturer is 
primarily responsible for any criminal offence the car performs when automated features are 
in control (Section 47 and 48)[4], it is questionable as to how responsive these companies will
be when these situations arise.
