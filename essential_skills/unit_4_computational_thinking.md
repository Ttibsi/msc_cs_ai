### Lesson 1 - Computational Thinking
Break down a task into smaller chunks that either a human or computer can understand

* Decomposition - Breaking down a complex problem into smaller, more managable parts
* Abstraction - Focusing only on the important information, ignoring irrelevant detail
* Pattern Recognition - Looking for similarities among and within problems
* Algorithms - Developing a step by step solution to a problem

Programming = telling a computer what to do and how to do it
Computational thinking = enabling you to work out exactly what to tell the computer
                        to do
