# Unit 3 - Online Study

* Evaluate and apply appropriate learning techniques for online, asynchronous master’s study (MLO 3)
* Apply time management techniques to create appropriate plans and to implement a personal study schedule to effectively engage in online, asynchronous learning (MLO 3)
* Evaluate and critically reflect on your learning strategies by evaluating their effectiveness and be able to adapt and improve these as appropriate (MLO 3)

### Lesson 1 - Studying Online
Important skills for online study:
* time management
* self discipline
* communication skills
* digital literacy
* self assessment and reflective thought
* planning
* prioritisation
* knowing when to take a break

### Lesson 2 - Time Management and Planning

* Create a schedule
* Prioritise tasks
* break down larger tasks into smaller manageable components
* Define short term and long term goals, set timelines to achieve them.
    - Identify specific, measurable, and achievable objectives.
* Plan - allocate time blocks for different activities or tasks

Pomodoro technique
25 minute focus sections, 5 minute breaks
After 4 focus sections, take a longer 30 min break
