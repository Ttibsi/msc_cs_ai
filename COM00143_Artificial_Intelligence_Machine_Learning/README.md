# COM00143M Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

Week 1: Introduction to Artificial Intelligence - Goals, Methods, Controversies
Week 2: Solving Problems with Search - Uninformed Search Strategies
Week 3: Solving Problems with Search - Informed Search Strategies
Week 4: Representing and Reasoning with Knowledge I
Week 5: Representing and Reasoning with Knowledge II
Week 6: Machine Learning with Clustering
Week 7: Machine Learning with Neural Networks

At the end of this module students will be able to:
* Critically analyse the principal ideas and techniques of artificial intelligence.
* Apply AI search to solve problems that may be represented as states, transitions and goals.
* Design logical systems that are able to represent knowledge and make decisions.
* Apply machine learning techniques to create AI agents that can learn from observed data.
* Critically evaluate the societal impact of AI including legal and ethical issues.
